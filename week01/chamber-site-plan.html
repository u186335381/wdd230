Valparaiso Chamber of Commerce. This name represents a city in which the chamber operates

The Valparaiso Chamber of Commerce serves as a gathering place for local businesses and companies who desire to increase their profit and reduce losses by gathering ideas to combat the increased bulgraries and robbings of the area, to request the government for increased security to maintain their businesses safe. Also they help one another with counseling to know what skills are most required to run a business, so that every business owner knows what knowledge could be most helpful to increase sales and reduce costs.
